Empowering Safer and Smarter Roads

Our Smart Traffic Monitoring System leverages cutting-edge AI technology to provide real-time insights into traffic conditions across Malabon City, starting with Barangay Longos C-4 Road.

Using Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W with CSI cameras and advanced machine learning models, we detect, classify, and analyze vehicle movements to help the local government make data-driven decisions for better traffic management.

Project Location

Currently monitoring traffic at Barangay Longos C-4 Road, with plans to expand across Malabon City based on successful implementation.

How It Works

AI processes live video feeds to detect vehicles, predict congestion, and generate recommendations for traffic management.

Core Features

Our system combines multiple advanced technologies to deliver comprehensive traffic insights.

Live AI Video Monitoring

RPi Zero 2 W with CSI Camera streams live video with real-time AI object detection for continuous traffic monitoring.

Vehicle Detection

Advanced AI models identify and classify vehicles in real-time, providing accurate traffic composition data.

Traffic Prediction

Facebook Prophet algorithm analyzes patterns to forecast traffic flow, enabling proactive congestion management.

AI Recommendations

Mistral LLM generates actionable traffic management suggestions based on historical and real-time data analysis.

Benefits for Malabon

How our Smart Traffic Monitoring System improves life in Malabon City

Reduced Congestion

AI-powered insights help optimize traffic flow, reducing frustrating delays during peak hours.

Faster Emergency Response

Real-time traffic data helps emergency vehicles find optimal routes, potentially saving lives.

Data-Driven Planning

Provides the LGU with accurate traffic data for infrastructure planning and policy decisions.

Safer Roads

Identifies accident-prone areas and suggests safety improvements to protect all road users.

Environmental Benefits

Reduced idling and optimized routes lead to lower emissions and better air quality.

Community Engagement

Transparent traffic data fosters trust between residents and local government.
